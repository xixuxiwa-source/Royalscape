About Royalscape

Founded in 2010, Royalscape has grown to become Entebbe's premier plant nursery and garden center. Our team of certified landscape architects and horticultural specialists are passionate about creating outdoor spaces that inspire and rejuvenate.

Located on Kitubulu Road in Entebbe, our nursery features an extensive collection of native and exotic plants, trees, and shrubs, all carefully nurtured to ensure they thrive in Ugandan climates.

We believe in a holistic approach to landscape design that balances aesthetic beauty with environmental responsibility.
